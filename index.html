Standard Curriculum

Kinder (3-6) Years

Kindergarten is typically for children aged 3 to 6 years old. It's a crucial period for early childhood development, focusing on play-based learning, social-emotional growth.

Elementary School

In elementary school, children embark on their educational journey, laying the foundation for future learning. Through engaging activities, stories, and games, they explore the world around them, develop essential literacy and numeracy skills

Middle (10-16) Years

Middle school science labs are a vital component of the educational experience for students aged 10-16. These labs provide a unique opportunity for students to engage with scientific concepts in a hands-on and interactive manner.
